ViewFinder Logo

For this project, I had to create a logo for a fictional app of my choice. I decided to create a logo for an app made for photographers. The app is a map for photographers to find new locations to photograph at. For the logo design, I took inspriation from a viewfinder on a camera body and thought the name fit perfectly for the app as well since these photographers are looking for more "views" to "find".

Grandma's Kitchen Cookbook

For this project, I created a cookbook full of family favorite recipies. My grandma always had crazy, but yuummy, recipies so I named it "Grandma's Kitchen" after her. This is a page layout from my personal favorite recipe.

Over Print Poster

For this project, I had to create a poster that graphic designers could relate to with an over print effect. I decided to base my project on the idea that graphic designers wear many hats. It is often that graphic designers are often called a million other names like, a photographer, a social media coordinator, a creative, and so much more. So, I thought this comment with the use of bright and fun colors would catch the attention of graphic designers.
